# Gram-Urban.AI

## AI-Powered District Digital Twin and Decision Support System

Gram-Urban.AI is an intelligent governance platform that transforms fragmented district administration into a unified, predictive, and data-driven decision support ecosystem.

It integrates citizen reports, departmental records, geospatial data, satellite insights, climate information, and public service data into a real-time digital twin of a district, helping administrators detect risks early, prioritize incidents, allocate resources efficiently, and make faster evidence-based decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

District administrations often rely on scattered data, manual reporting, delayed communication, and reactive decision-making.

Citizen complaints, healthcare alerts, agricultural issues, infrastructure failures, pollution reports, and disaster warnings are usually handled separately by different departments. This creates delays, duplication of effort, poor resource allocation, and missed early warning signals.

Gram-Urban.AI solves this by creating a single intelligence layer where every report, dataset, and location-based event is analyzed together.

---

## Our Solution

Gram-Urban.AI acts as a live digital twin of a district.

Citizens can report local issues through a mobile-first portal using text, images, voice, and GPS-tagged locations. The system uses Gemini’s multimodal AI capabilities to analyze reports, extract meaningful insights, classify issues, detect severity, and correlate them with existing government and environmental datasets.

Administrators receive real-time recommendations through an interactive dashboard powered by AI analytics, GIS visualization, climate monitoring, and decision intelligence.

---

## Key Features

### Citizen Reporting Portal

Citizens can submit reports using:

- Text-based complaints
- Images of incidents
- Voice inputs
- GPS-tagged locations
- Category-based issue reporting

This enables quick and inclusive participation from both urban and rural citizens.

---

### Multimodal AI Report Analysis

Gemini AI analyzes citizen-generated reports and extracts:

- Issue category
- Severity level
- Location context
- Visual evidence
- Urgency score
- Department mapping
- Recommended action

This reduces manual verification and speeds up administrative response.

---

### District Digital Twin

The platform builds a live digital representation of the district using:

- Geospatial information
- Citizen reports
- Departmental records
- Satellite and climate data
- Infrastructure data
- Healthcare and public service indicators

This helps administrators understand the current state of the district in real time.

---

### AI-Based Decision Engine

The decision engine prioritizes incidents based on:

- Severity
- Public impact
- Number of affected citizens
- Location sensitivity
- Available resources
- Cross-departmental dependencies
- Climate and environmental risks

It generates actionable recommendations for faster governance decisions.

---

### Inter-Departmental Coordination

Gram-Urban.AI connects multiple governance domains, including:

- Healthcare
- Agriculture
- Infrastructure
- Pollution control
- Disaster management
- Public services
- Water and sanitation
- Urban and rural development

Instead of treating issues as isolated complaints, the platform identifies hidden relationships across departments.

---

### Administrator Dashboard

The dashboard provides:

- Live incident tracking
- GIS-based district visualization
- AI-generated recommendations
- Climate and disaster monitoring
- Department-wise issue mapping
- Priority-based task queues
- Real-time analytics
- Resource allocation insights

This helps administrators move from manual monitoring to intelligent decision-making.
